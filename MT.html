MISSION

At the Metro Chronicle, our mission is to be the most trusted source for local news in our community. We are dedicated to providing accurate, unbiased, and in-depth reporting that empowers our readers to stay informed and engaged with the events shaping their city.


OUR STORY

Founded in 2015 by a group of local journalists frustrated by the decline of comprehensive local news coverage, the Metro Chronicle was created with a single goal: to fill the void. We believe that a strong community is built on shared information, and we are committed to delivering the stories that matter most to our neighbors—from city council meetings and school board decisions to the vibrant culture and community events that make our city unique.


WHAT WE DO

Our team is comprised of experienced journalists, editors, and photographers who are passionate about our city. We are your neighbors, and we are committed to providing a high level of journalistic integrity. You can meet some of our key team members [link to team page].


WHAT MAKE US DIFFRENT

In an age of information overload, we focus on providing context and depth. We don't just report the headlines; we dig deeper to explain the "why" behind the story. Our commitment is to you, our readers, and to building a stronger, more informed community together.
