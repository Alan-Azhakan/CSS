Electric Cars Revolution

Electric cars (EVs) use one or more electric motors to be propelled by electricity, which is stored in a rechargeable battery. They offer environmental benefits like zero tailpipe emissions, quieter operation, and lower maintenance due to fewer moving parts compared to gasoline cars. EVs can be categorized into several types, including battery-electric vehicles (BEVs) which run entirely on electricity, and plug-in hybrid electric vehicles (PHEVs) which combine a gasoline engine with an electric motor and battery.
